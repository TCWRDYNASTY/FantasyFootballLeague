Official League Constitution
Updated March 2025

General Rules & Structure

1. Entry and Fees

  • $25 buy-in fee per season.
  • $25 buyout fee if a manager decides to leave the league.
  • All buyout money will be added to the following year‚Äôs winning pot, increasing the stakes!

2. Winning Pot Breakdown

  • First place: $205
  • Second place: $50
  • Third place: $25
  • $70 from the pot is allocated for trophy maintenance, covering engraving, shipping to the current champion, and nameplate updates. Any remaining funds from this allocation will be awarded to the previous year‚Äôs champion.

*Note: Any buyout fees collected will be split evenly among the first, second, and third-place winners.

3. League Size

  • 14 teams, operating without divisions.

4. Playoffs

  • A 6-team playoff format will be used.
  • The first and second-place teams will receive first-round byes.
  • Playoff seeding follows a highest-seed vs. lowest-seed format.
  • Playoffs officially kick off in Week 15 of the NFL season.

5. Trade Deadline

  • The trade deadline is set for Week 11.
  • All accepted trades will be processed; there are no trade vetoes.
  • Trades will be reviewed and processed within 1 day to ensure timely roster updates.

6. Waivers

  • The league utilizes rolling continuous waivers.

7. New League Year Timeline

  • The trade window opens at the start of the NFL league year (typically in March).
  • The rookie draft commences one day after the actual NFL Draft and will run over the weekend, with adjustable pick times to accommodate all managers.
  • Free agents can be added to rosters one week after the rookie draft concludes.

8. Rookie Draft

  • A three-round linear draft.
  • Draft order follows an NBA-style weighted lottery:
    • Teams ranked 7‚Äì14 will be entered into a lottery for the first overall pick, with the highest odds going to the team that finishes in last place (14th).
    • Teams ranked 1‚Äì6 will be placed at the back of the draft and are excluded from the lottery, with the 1st place team receiving the 14th overall pick.
  • The final draft order will be released during Super Bowl weekend.
  • **Lottery Odds:**
    • 14th place = 21% chance at 1st overall
    • 13th place = 18% chance at 1st overall
    • 12th place = 16% chance at 1st overall
    • 11th place = 14% chance at 1st overall
    • 10th place = 12% chance at 1st overall
    • 9th place = 8% chance at 1st overall
    • 8th place = 6% chance at 1st overall
    • 7th place = 5% chance at 1st overall

9. Roster Composition

  • 1 Starting QB
  • 2 Starting RB
  • 2 Starting WR
  • 1 Starting TE
  • 1 Starting Flex (TE/WR/RB)
  • 1 Starting Defense
  • 1 Starting Kicker
  • 10 Bench Spots
  • 3 Taxi Squad Spots
  • 4 IR Spots

10. Taxi Squad Eligibility

  • Only rookies with less than one year of NFL experience are eligible for the taxi squad.
  • Eligible players can be moved to taxi squad spots at any point during the season.
